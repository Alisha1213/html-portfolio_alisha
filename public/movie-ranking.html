My top three movies of all time.


Kung Fu Panda

It is insipiring movies showcasing hardwork and resillience, one can become master of things they desire.

Reply 1998

A korean drama series which showcase family relations, values and society in an asian culture during time period of 1980s. The show revolves around five friends from same neighbourhood who grew together. Their friendship and bond show beautiful friendship and love shared among the neighbours treating like one big family.

The Karate Kid

One of the iconic movie about a little boy living in United States travelled to China for new start but bullying lead him to learn kunfu as a mechanism for defence. His hardwork and dedication makes him a karate champion proving human can shape themselves into things they desire.
